Purpose

The Research Purpose

To investigate the tension between academic integrity and AI-driven productivity. By analyzing 21 survey responses from students and TAs, this project looks for a sustainable middle ground for the future of the classroom.

02

Experience

A Dialogue Experience

Moving away from combative debate, this podcast uses the Art of Empathy to explore stakeholder fears. It is built as a brave space where skeptical professors and tech-reliant students can both feel heard and understood.

03

Focus

Illuminating Solutions

The focus is regulation over banning. The data points toward clear guidelines and stronger AI literacy, with an emphasis on using technology to enhance critical thinking rather than replace it.

About The Project

The rhetoric behind the project matters as much as the topic.

This project was created for WRT 205 at Syracuse University. It utilizes the Art of Empathy to bridge the gap between frustrated educators and overwhelmed students. By moving from a debate of "right vs. wrong" to a dialogue of "how do we coexist," I hope to illuminate a path for responsible AI use in the classroom.
